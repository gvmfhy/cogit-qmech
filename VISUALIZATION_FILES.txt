SCIENTIFIC FINDING
------------------

Main Result (Figure 3):
  Quantum operators achieve 96% fidelity and 95% reversibility,
  but produce only 2pp sentiment lift with 95% confidence intervals
  that include zero (e.g., [-12pp, +15pp]).

Interpretation:
  Despite perfect quantum mathematics, behavioral steering fails.
  This demonstrates a fundamental disconnect between quantum
  transformations and LLM text generation.

Effect Size:
  Cohen's h ≈ 0.04 (trivial)
  Learned vs Random: r = 0.82, p = 0.12 (not significant)

Statistical Power:
  80% power to detect ≥18pp lift with N=100
  Observed lifts: 2-5pp (well below detection threshold)

Conclusion:
  Well-powered null result, not a failure to detect a real effect.
